Intro

Over the course of my Data Analytics journey, I have learned to transform raw data into actionable items by way of Excel Modeling, querying SQL databases, interactive visualizations in Tableau and Power BI, and tying it all together with Python. Check out some of my work! Currently, I am creating tools using Power BI that leverage sales and product data to deliver important insights to company stakeholders and decision makers. I am also taking part in a machine learning pilot project, which aims to predict the net retention of customers as well as insights regarding possible expansion opportunities, or churn warnings. In the future, I would like to dive deeper into the world of machine learning and automation using Python - I believe it will be an essential to any data-driven company!

T-Tests in Excel

This project dives into housing data while act as an analyst at an investment bank. The team wants to understand how they should allocate dollars earmarked for investment into mortgage-backed securities. In this spreadsheet accompanied with a presentation, I explore factors driving home prices using t-tests with the given data set and make reccommendations for the bank to invest.
